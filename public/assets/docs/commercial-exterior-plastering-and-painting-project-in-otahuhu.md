# Commercial Exterior Plastering and Painting Project in Otahuhu

Exterior cracks and aging coatings can quickly become larger issues when left untreated, especially on commercial properties exposed to constant weather conditions.

This Otahuhu project involved a commercial exterior renovation focused on restoring the appearance of the building while improving long-term protection against moisture intrusion and surface deterioration.

While the surfaces were not heavily damaged overall, visible cracking throughout the exterior had already begun allowing water penetration in certain areas. The goal was to properly stabilise the surfaces, reinforce the plaster system, and apply a durable coating solution designed for long-term performance.

  

## Scope of Work

-   Full exterior wash and surface cleaning
-   Removal of loose plaster and contaminants
-   Crack repairs and preparation
-   Key coat application
-   Full mesh reinforcement system installation
-   Resitex texture coating application
-   Resene X200 protective paint system

  

## Reinforcing Exterior Surfaces Properly

One of the most important stages of this project involved reinforcing vulnerable exterior areas before the finishing system was applied.

Rather than simply patching visible cracks, the property received a full mesh reinforcement system designed to improve strength and help minimise future cracking movement.

Surface preparation also included waterblasting and cleaning to remove contaminants and unstable material prior to the installation of the new plaster and coating system.

At PlasterPro Solution, preparation and reinforcement are key parts of achieving long-lasting exterior results.

  

## Commercial Exterior Protection

Commercial properties require exterior systems that not only improve appearance but continue performing under constant exposure to weather conditions.

For this project, the selected coating system was designed to provide both a refreshed visual finish and improved protection against future moisture-related issues.

The combination of reinforced plastering, texture coating, and weather-resistant paint created a far more stable and durable exterior surface across the property.

  

## A Stronger and Cleaner Exterior Finish

Once completed, the building presented with a far cleaner appearance and a significantly more consistent exterior finish.

The reinforced plaster system and protective coatings now provide a stronger foundation for long term durability while also improving the overall presentation of the commercial property.
