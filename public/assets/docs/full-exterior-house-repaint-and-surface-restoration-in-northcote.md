# Full Exterior House Repaint and Surface Restoration in Northcote

When a property goes years without proper exterior maintenance, the damage eventually becomes impossible to ignore.

That was the situation with this Northcote home.

The exterior surfaces had deteriorated heavily over time due to sun exposure, aging coatings, and lack of maintenance. Large areas of paint were peeling and flaking, timber surfaces were beginning to fail, and the overall presentation of the property had declined significantly.

The homeowners were preparing the property for sale and wanted the work completed properly — not simply covered up. The goal was to restore the home’s appearance, improve long-term protection, and ensure the property presented at a much higher standard before going to market.


## Scope of Work

-   Full exterior house wash
-   Detailed surface preparation
-   Removal of loose and failing paint
-   Timber repairs and preparation
-   Exterior sanding and remediation
-   Full exterior repainting
-   Protective Resene coating system application

  

## Why Preparation Matters Before Repainting

One of the biggest mistakes in exterior repainting is rushing directly into the paint application stage without properly preparing the surfaces underneath.

On this project, preparation was the most important part of the process.

Years of sun exposure had caused widespread paint failure across multiple areas of the home. Before repainting could begin, the exterior surfaces needed to be carefully cleaned, stripped back, repaired, and stabilised to ensure the new coating system would perform correctly.

Our team carried out a full exterior wash and detailed preparation process to remove contaminants, loose coatings, and damaged material prior to repainting. Timber areas also required additional attention due to weathering and deterioration caused by long-term exposure.

At PlasterPro Solution, we believe durable finishes are built through preparation first — not shortcuts.

  

## Repainting a Property for Sale

This project also had a strong real estate focus.

When preparing a home for sale, exterior presentation can have a major impact on first impressions. Tired paintwork, peeling surfaces, and neglected details can quickly affect how buyers perceive the condition of a property overall.

The homeowners wanted a result that felt clean, fresh, and professionally completed while also improving the long-term protection of the home itself.

Using quality Resene products and a preparation-first approach, the property was completely transformed from worn and weathered to sharp, clean, and market-ready.

  

## A Complete Exterior Transformation

By the end of the project, the difference across the property was substantial.

The refreshed exterior now delivers a far cleaner appearance, stronger protection against future weather exposure, and a finish that presents confidently both in person and in property marketing photography.

Projects like this are a reminder that great repainting work starts long before the final coat is applied.
