# Coastal New Build Plastering and Painting Project on Waiheke Island

Working on island properties requires a different level of planning, coordination, and preparation.

This Waiheke Island project involved a full plastering and painting system for a newly built residential home designed to handle the demands of a coastal environment while delivering a clean, high-quality finish throughout the property.

From logistics and transport coordination through to the finishing stages, every part of the project required careful management to achieve the final result.

  

## Scope of Work

-   Full exterior plastering
-   Full painting system application
-   Surface preparation and finishing
-   Coastal protection-focused coatings
-   New-build exterior finishing work
-   Detailed workmanship and finishing

  

## Building for Coastal Conditions

Coastal homes face constant exposure to salt air, moisture, strong sun, and changing weather conditions.

Because of this, durability and preparation become essential parts of the plastering and painting process.

For this project, the focus was not only on presentation but also on applying systems designed to protect the home long term within an exposed island environment.

  

## Managing Island Logistics and Workmanship Standards

Projects on Waiheke Island require additional coordination compared to standard Auckland residential works.

Transporting materials, managing schedules, and maintaining workflow consistency all become more important when working remotely from the mainland.

Despite the additional logistical challenges, the project was completed with a strong focus on workmanship, detail, and finish quality throughout every stage.

  

## A Clean and Durable Coastal Finish

By the end of the project, the new home presented with smooth plaster finishes, clean painted surfaces, and a coating system designed for long-term performance in coastal conditions.

Projects like this highlight the importance of combining preparation, planning, and quality workmanship when working on high-exposure residential properties.
