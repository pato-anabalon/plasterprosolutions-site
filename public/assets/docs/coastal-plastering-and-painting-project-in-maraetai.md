# Coastal Plastering and Painting Project in Maraetai

Building and finishing exterior surfaces in coastal environments requires a much higher level of preparation, durability, and attention to detail.

This Maraetai project involved a newly built home located directly on the waterfront, where long-term protection against weather exposure was just as important as the final appearance.

Our team was brought in to complete exterior plastering and painting works across the property, including concrete walls, pool areas, stairs, and exterior surfaces designed to handle the demands of a harsh coastal environment.

## Scope of Work

-   Exterior plastering
-   Concrete wall finishing
-   Pool area plastering and painting
-   Stair surface preparation and finishing
-   Exterior painting system application
-   Detailed surface preparation
-   Coastal protection-focused finishing work

  

## Working in a Coastal Environment

Properties located near the ocean face constant exposure to moisture, salt air, wind, and strong UV conditions.

Because of this, preparation and workmanship become critical to the long-term performance of exterior plaster and paint systems.

On this project, careful attention was given to the consistency of the plaster finish, surface detailing, and coating application process to ensure the completed work would not only look sharp but continue performing over time.

The project also required strong coordination due to the location and high expectations surrounding the property itself.

  

## Premium Exterior Finishes

The homeowners were looking for a result that reflected the quality of the build overall.

From the pool surrounds to the exterior walls, the focus throughout the project was on clean finishes, durable workmanship, and consistency across every visible surface.

Working across multiple concrete areas required careful preparation and finishing methods to maintain a cohesive appearance throughout the property.

  

## A Coastal Home Designed to Last

By the end of the project, the home presented with smooth exterior finishes, clean lines, and a protective coating system suited to the coastal environment surrounding the property.

Projects like this highlight the importance of combining proper preparation with high-quality workmanship, especially when working on exposed waterfront homes across Auckland.
