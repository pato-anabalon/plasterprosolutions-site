# Exterior Plastering and Painting Renovation in Ellerslie

Over time, exterior surfaces naturally begin to show signs of wear, especially when older coatings and plaster finishes start deteriorating.

This Ellerslie project involved a full exterior plastering and painting renovation designed to restore tired surfaces and improve the overall appearance of the property.

The works included both the exterior areas of the home as well as the interior and exterior garage walls connected to the property.

  

## Scope of Work

-   Exterior plastering repairs
-   Full exterior painting
-   Garage wall plastering and repainting
-   Interior garage wall finishing
-   Surface preparation and restoration
-   Residential renovation work

  

## Restoring Exterior Surfaces Properly

Before repainting could begin, the existing surfaces required detailed preparation and plaster restoration.

Older surfaces showing signs of wear often require far more than a simple repaint to achieve a consistent finish. Preparation and plastering work help create a stable base that improves both appearance and long-term durability.

On this project, our team focused on restoring the condition of the exterior walls and garage surfaces before the final coating stages began.

  

## Creating Consistent Finishes Across the Property

One of the key goals of this renovation was achieving consistency between the main exterior areas and the attached garage spaces.

By completing plastering and painting works across both interior and exterior garage surfaces, the final result felt far more cohesive throughout the property.

Attention to detail during preparation and finishing helped create smoother surfaces and a cleaner final presentation overall.

  

## A Refreshed Residential Exterior

Once completed, the property presented with a significantly cleaner and more updated appearance.

The restored plastering and repainting works improved both the look and protection of the home while helping bring new life back to aging surfaces.
